## 🎨 Color Scheme Explained

I chose the colors carefully to match a cozy coffee shop atmosphere:

- `#4B3832` (Dark Brown) – used for the header and footer; represents rich coffee tones  
- `#D9CAB3` (Beige/Cream) – a warm, soft background color that resembles milk and creates a cozy feeling  
- `#FFE6BC` (Light Cream) – used for accents and hover effects, giving a buttery smooth touch  
- `#3C2F2F` (Dark Chocolate) – used for text and titles, providing strong contrast for readability  
- `#C97C5D` (Cinnamon/Caramel) – highlights and buttons, adding a pop of color with warmth  

These colors are inspired by real coffee drinks: espresso, latte, cream, and caramel.
